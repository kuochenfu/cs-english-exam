# Phonics — Prefixes re-, un-, and dis-

Source: `raw/phonics03.JPG` (Show-It workbook pp. 134 & 139)

## Rule
A **prefix** is a word part that appears before a root word and changes the root word's meaning.
- **re-** = again (e.g., *refill* = fill again)
- **un-** = not / opposite of (e.g., *unsafe* = not safe)
- **dis-** = not / opposite of (e.g., *dislike* = not like)

## Word Bank
| re- | un- | dis- |
|---|---|---|
| refill, repaid, recount, repaint, reread, resell, readmit, regrow | unsafe, untidy, untrue, unlock, unfold, unhappy, unknown, unclasp, untie | dislike, distaste, disagree, discharge, disbelief |

## Sentence Prompts
> Some people think it is unimportant to recycle cans and bottles.

> Mathias had to unbutton his shirt before taking it off.

## Poem — "The Secret Place" by Dennis Lee

There's a place I know inside myself,
Where nobody else can be,
And nobody else can tell me there's —
Nobody knows but me.

It's hard to explain the way it feels,
Or even where I go.
It isn't a place in time or space,
But once I'm there, I know.

There's a place I know inside myself,
And it's shiny and new and small,
And it's neither big nor small,
And whenever I go, it feels as though
I never left at all.
