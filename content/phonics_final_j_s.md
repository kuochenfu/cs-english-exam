# Phonics — Words with Final /j/ and /s/

Source: `raw/phonics02.JPG` (Show-It workbook pp. 126 & 130)

## Rule
The final **/j/** sound can be spelled **ge** or **dge**.
The final **/s/** sound can be spelled **s**, **ss**, **ce**, or **se**.

## Word Bank
| Final /j/ (ge / dge) | Final /s/ (ce / ss / s) |
|---|---|
| badge, storage, cage, knowledge, postage, passage, bridge, cabbage, strange, arrange, judge, image | prince, once, presence, ice, France, grass, slice, retrace, sequence, sauce, twice, necklace, lettuce |

## Sentence Prompts
> Alice put the postage stamp on the envelope to send it to France.

> Some strange vegetables, like cabbage and lettuce, grow in round head shapes.
